# 👋 Interactive Portfolio – Musharib

Welcome to my interactive portfolio.

This isn't just another portfolio website—it's an experiment in making the web feel alive.

## ✨ About

Hi, I'm **Musharib**.

I build playful things on the internet—interfaces that feel less like software and more like something you can touch.

The hero section features an interactive hand-controlled experience. The video isn't driven by a traditional animation. Instead, it's scrubbed in real time based on your hand gesture.

👊 Make a fist → The animation closes and the thoughts disappear.

🖐️ Open your palm → The animation plays and my thoughts come to life.

The goal is to create a portfolio that people don't just scroll through—they interact with it.

## 🎮 How It Works

1. Allow camera access.
2. The browser detects your hand using MediaPipe.
3. Open your hand to scrub the hero animation forward.
4. Close your fist to scrub it backward.
5. Explore the rest of the portfolio.
